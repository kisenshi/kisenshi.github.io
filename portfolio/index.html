Code-Your-Own-Adventure

Prototype built with Ruby Sinatra and Bootstrap for the IWDHackathon organised by Talented Heads the weekend of the 7th and 8th of March of 2015. The theme was Make it Happen – Diversity in Tech. The winning solution developed was a story telling platform to teach children coding basics and encourage them to keep trying until they are able to create the perfect story.

For more details, refer to this blog entrance.

project_euler_solutions

My solutions to Project Euler problems. For those who don´t know, Project Euler is an online platform that contains lot of problems to solve. I like solving problems and I thought it was a fun way to start using Python and get familiar with the language.

Please note that I publish in here my solutions, if you are using the platform I recommend not to spoil yourself, give a try and solve them in your own; where is the fun in using my solutions as yours?

hackerrank_solutions

My solutions to Hackerrank challenges, which is another cool platform with lot of problems related with algorithms, statistics, machine learning, natural language processing… and contests.

Please try to solve the problems on your own; do not use my solutions as yours, where would be the fun on that?
