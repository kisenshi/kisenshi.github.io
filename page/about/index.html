A little bit about me

Hello world!

I’m Cris and Cheese with posts is my blog.

I am a Computer Engineer in love with London, where I’ve been living since 2013. Although I had always enjoyed creative tasks as drawing and painting; I opted for a more technical career and I absolutely love it! I am passionate about solving problems, coding and the feel when something you have built actually works!

I like travelling and, luckily, I have had the chance to travel a lot since I was a kid; I’ve been in many countries and visited amazing places; but I still have a huge list of places to go that keeps growing every day! I love music, TV series, cinema and discovering new places in the city!

You might be wondering why the name… Well, as I’ll write about different things: technology, my experiences, curiosities (basically all I feel like sharing with the world), I needed a general name, something different, original, easy to remember… and everything tastes better with cheese; so it was perfect adding it to my blog as well!

Thanks for reading!
