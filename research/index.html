Research: Game Testing in Industry Interviews

Note: I am not actively running these interviews anymore, but if you are interested on providing your feedback or have any questions, please feel free to drop me a line to c.guerreroromero[at]qmul.ac.uk

Do you work in the games industry?

I am a PhD student at the Queen Mary University of London, and my research focuses on using a team of Artificial Intelligence (AI) agents with different play-styles for automatic testing.

I am running interviews to better understand what the games industry is looking for when testing a game, either when using human testers or automatic testing, and how these processes are carried out.

The purpose of these interviews is to collect data that will shape the implementation of my experiments and future studies. Check the information sheet for more detailed information.

The interview process consists on a series of questions related to game testing, including the procedures carried out by each company in the industry (both when using human playtesters and automatic testing). The questions are also designed to find the shortfalls in the current approaches and how they could be improved.

These interviews can be done in person or filling out an online form, based on your availability and preference.
