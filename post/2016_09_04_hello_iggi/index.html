Hello IGGI!

Wed Sep 14, 2016 in IGGI
#IGGI #gamesresearch #PhD

I am really excited to finally say Hello IGGI as I am one of the new students in the University of Essex! It is a great opportunity and I can’t wait to see what comes out of the next 4 years!

But wait… What is IGGI??


Right! I’ll explain!

The Intelligent Games and Game Intelligence (IGGI) PhD programme is the biggest fully funded research programme in Artificial Intelligence and Games in the world. It is sponsored by EPSRC and possible thanks to the collaboration of three institutions: University of Essex, Goldsmiths University of London and University of York. For detailed information refer to the IGGI site.
